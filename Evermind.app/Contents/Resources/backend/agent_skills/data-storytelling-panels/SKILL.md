DATA STORYTELLING PANELS

Dashboards should tell the operator what changed and what matters.

- Lead with 3-4 KPI cards that answer performance, risk, and trend at a glance.
- Use charts to show movement, not to restate table values.
- Filters must change visible values or states, otherwise they are fake controls.
- Each panel should have a job: monitor, compare, diagnose, or decide.
- If a chart exists, label the takeaway, not just the metric name.
