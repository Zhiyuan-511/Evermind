LOTTIE READINESS

Motion should be portable, lightweight, and intentional.

- Favor simple transform-based motion that can later be encoded as Lottie-like sequences.
- Use layered timing: entry, settle, idle.
- Avoid random animation spam; every motion should signal hierarchy, progress, or delight.
- Keep loops subtle and non-blocking.
- If external motion assets are unavailable, define animation states precisely enough to recreate later.
