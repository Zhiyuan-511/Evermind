EVIDENCE-DRIVEN QA

Approval requires evidence, not intuition.

For every meaningful interaction, keep a tiny evidence ledger:
- target you acted on
- what the page showed before
- what action you took
- what changed after
- whether any console/page/network errors appeared

Minimum evidence before APPROVED:
- website/dashboard: at least 2 evidence items, including 1 interaction that visibly changes the UI
- game: start/play transition plus gameplay evidence showing controls changed score/HUD/scene state

If you cannot produce evidence for a claimed behavior:
- do not guess
- do not approve
- output REJECTED and explain what could not be verified
