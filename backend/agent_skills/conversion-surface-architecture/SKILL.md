CONVERSION SURFACE ARCHITECTURE

Marketing pages win when the path to action is obvious.

- Establish one primary action for the page and make it visually dominant.
- Every major section should answer one of: why trust, why care, why now, what next.
- Support the CTA with proof: metrics, testimonials, customer logos, demos, guarantees.
- Remove duplicate weak CTAs; keep one strong hierarchy.
- If the page feels beautiful but does not convert, it is unfinished.
